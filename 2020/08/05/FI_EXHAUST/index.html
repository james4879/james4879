FI EXHAUST

Author: Same! Date: August 5, 2020  8:47:13

Have you ever tried distinguishing between different car models just by listening?

Number of cylinders, materials and designs, and firing order, creates their completely different characters.

Let’ play a game and see only through sound wave if you can tell what the exact car models of the three following cars.

Whether you can answer correctly or not, you can actually feel the difference among these car models.

Just like brass instruments all the sound is produced by a tubular resonator then transmitted to our ears.

And therefore the exhaust pipe crucially becomes the final factor of the sound quality.

Among hundreds of exhaust brands the Occidentally renowned FI EXHAUST has always been leaving me a solitary and mysterious impression.

You might feel that a valve is a structure simply for on-off control.

But in our concept now that we have the on-off control functionality, we combine two sets of different attributes of the exhaust together into a single one.

In a limited space, we put two products that are completely opposite together by utilizing the basic feature of the valve.

Because through the whole market as for as I know the main purpose of a value in an exhausts system is volume control.

If you pick up whatever brands of exhausts on the market they all suffer an alternative problem, either torque performance or horsepower.

Usually by enlarging the diameter of the pipe, your horsepower will improve at the case of some torque.

While if you do the opposite, your torque performance will improve at the cost of horsepower.

Now if we utilize the switching feature of a valve and combine it with our system,

Just lie that as we all know, most cars about 90% are turbocharged.

Now in our system, we add a new feature called turbo boost.

This turbine is not yet turned on, and we can use our system to do the job.

When the turbine is off, it run in a high torque performance way.

As the turbine kicks in, you don’t need torque any more because the turbine has started to pressurize all you need is horsepower.

At this time the system will do a simple switch using the valve to a high horsepower mode.

And you can personally customize when to kick in with our system.

At 0.1 bar, 0.2 bar or whatever, according to the test data you can set whichever you want.

Different cars have different rang of modification.

Thus, every owner, every car needs fine adjustment by the owner himself and that’s also another pleasure of car modification.

Everyday you can go and try it out try covering a 1/4 mile or 100-200 KPH acceleration 0-100 KPH would also be great if you like.

Then choose the best performance data for your car.
